# Michael Saylor — Bitcoin Treasury Framework

## Background
MicroStrategy CEO who converted company treasury to Bitcoin starting August 2020. Has purchased over 500,000 Bitcoin for MicroStrategy, making it the largest corporate Bitcoin holder in history. Most prominent institutional Bitcoin advocate and educator.

## Core Philosophy
Bitcoin is the apex property of the human race — digital energy that cannot be confiscated, inflated, or destroyed. Every other asset depreciates against Bitcoin over time. The only rational question is how much Bitcoin to own and how to acquire more. Time in the market beats timing the market specifically for Bitcoin.

## The Four Properties That Make Bitcoin Unique
1. **Scarcity** — 21 million hard cap, mathematically enforced
2. **Durability** — exists on 50,000+ nodes globally, no single point of failure
3. **Divisibility** — 100 million satoshis per Bitcoin, infinite divisibility
4. **Portability** — can move $1 billion across borders in 10 minutes with no intermediary

## Bitcoin vs Other Asset Classes (Saylor's Framework)
- **vs Gold**: Bitcoin wins on portability, divisibility, verifiability, and network effects. Gold is analog Bitcoin.
- **vs Real Estate**: Bitcoin wins on liquidity and lack of counterparty risk. Real estate is local, illiquid, and taxed.
- **vs Equities**: Bitcoin wins on simplicity. No earnings manipulation, no dilution, no management risk.
- **vs Bonds**: Bonds are guaranteed losers against inflation. Bitcoin is the long bond on human civilization.
- **vs Cash**: Cash is a melting ice cube. Bitcoin is fire.

## The Corporate Treasury Thesis
Traditional corporate treasury in cash = guaranteed loss of purchasing power
Bitcoin treasury = hardest money ever created
Risk of holding Bitcoin long term << Risk of NOT holding Bitcoin long term

**MicroStrategy formula:**
- Issue convertible notes or equity to buy Bitcoin
- Bitcoin appreciates faster than cost of debt
- Net Asset Value grows even if stock price lags
- Creates "Bitcoin yield" — BTC per share metric

## Institutional Adoption Framework
Watch in this order:
1. **Individual** whales → early adopter phase (2009-2017)
2. **Family offices** → wealth preservation phase (2018-2020)
3. **Public companies** → treasury phase (2020-2022) — MicroStrategy started this
4. **Sovereign wealth funds** → nation-state phase (2024+)
5. **Central banks** → reserve asset phase (inevitable)

Each wave is 10x larger than the last.

## Bitcoin Volatility as a Feature
Volatility is not risk — volatility is opportunity.
Bitcoin is volatile because it is discovering its price as the world's first perfectly scarce digital asset.
As Bitcoin market cap approaches gold ($13T+), volatility will decrease.
Volatility premium today = extraordinary returns for early adopters.
"If you don't like volatility, you don't deserve the returns."
