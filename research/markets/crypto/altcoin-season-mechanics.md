# Altcoin Season Mechanics

## How Altcoin Season Works
Altcoin season is when altcoins outperform Bitcoin. It follows a predictable rotation driven by risk appetite, leverage availability, and narrative cycling.

## The Rotation Sequence
1. **Bitcoin (months 1-6 of bull):** Institutional quality. ETF flows. Clearest narrative. Least volatile entry for new money.
2. **Ethereum (months 3-9):** DeFi backbone. Smart contract platform. Needed for altcoin ecosystem to function.
3. **Large-cap altcoins (months 6-12):** SOL, AVAX, DOT, LINK, etc. Proven use cases, liquid markets.
4. **Mid-cap DeFi/L2 (months 9-15):** ARB, OP, UNI, AAVE, etc. Ecosystem tokens.
5. **Small-cap narrative plays (months 12-18):** AI tokens, gaming, memes — whatever narrative is hot.
6. **Micro-caps and memes (final weeks):** Maximum speculation, maximum risk.

**Peak = when micro-caps are making 50x in a week and mainstream headlines cover random meme coins.**

## Bitcoin Dominance (BTC.D) Signal
**The most reliable altcoin season indicator:**
- BTC.D above 55% rising: Bitcoin season, stay in BTC
- BTC.D 50-55% and topping: Transition phase, begin ETH
- BTC.D falling through 50%: Altcoin season starting
- BTC.D below 45% falling: Full altcoin season
- BTC.D below 40%: Late cycle, reduce altcoin exposure

## ETH/BTC Ratio
When ETH starts outperforming Bitcoin (ETH/BTC ratio rising):
- Signals smart money rotating into alts
- ETH leads altcoin season — if ETH not moving vs BTC, altcoins won't either
- ETH/BTC ratio all-time high = altcoin season peak historically

## Identifying the Altcoin Season Catalyst
Each cycle has a dominant narrative:
- 2017: ICOs and "blockchain everything"
- 2020-2021: DeFi summer → NFT boom
- 2024-2025: ETFs, AI tokens, RWA (Real World Assets), meme coins on Solana

**How to spot the next narrative:**
- Developer activity (GitHub commits) in a sector
- VC investment announcements
- Protocol launches with major backers
- Influencer/KOL focus shifting

## Altcoin Risk Management
**The 80/20 rule:**
- 80% in Bitcoin and Ethereum (relatively safer)
- 20% in high-conviction altcoins
- Never >5% in any single small-cap

**Volatility expectations:**
- Large cap alts: 3-5x Bitcoin's volatility
- Mid cap alts: 5-10x Bitcoin's volatility
- Small cap alts: 10-30x Bitcoin's volatility

**Exit strategy:** Define price targets before entry. Altcoins rarely give graceful exits — be ready to sell into strength.

## Altcoin Sector Analysis
**Layer 1 blockchains (SOL, AVAX, NEAR, SUI, APT):**
- Compete with Ethereum for developer share
- Key metric: developer count, DeFi TVL, transaction volume
- Risk: "tech is better" doesn't guarantee price performance

**Layer 2 (ARB, OP, MATIC):**
- Grow with Ethereum adoption
- Key metric: L2 TVL, transaction volume
- More predictable than L1 competitors

**DeFi protocols (UNI, AAVE, CRV, MKR):**
- Revenue-generating protocols
- Key metric: fee revenue, protocol growth
- More fundamentally valued than pure narrative plays

**Infrastructure (LINK, GRT, FIL):**
- Enable other protocols to function
- Slow but steady adoption

**AI + Crypto (FET, RENDER, TAO):**
- 2024-2025 dominant narrative
- Speculative, most are infrastructure with no users yet
- High narrative risk

**Meme coins (DOGE, SHIB, PEPE, WIF):**
- Pure sentiment plays — no fundamentals
- Can 100x and -99% in same cycle
- Size accordingly (treat as lottery tickets)
