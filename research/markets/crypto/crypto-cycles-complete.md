# Crypto Market Cycles — Complete Guide

## The Four-Year Bitcoin Halving Cycle
Bitcoin's supply schedule creates predictable scarcity events every ~4 years.

**Historical cycle pattern:**
| Phase | Timing | Characteristics |
|-------|--------|----------------|
| Accumulation | Year 1-2 post peak | Low prices, despair, smart money buys |
| Early Bull | 12-18 months pre-halving | Slow grind up, low retail interest |
| Halving | Every ~4 years | Supply cut 50%, narrative focus |
| Expansion | 6-18 months post-halving | Price acceleration, institutional entry |
| Blow-off Top | 18-24 months post-halving | Retail FOMO, parabolic, euphoria |
| Bear Market | 12-24 months | 70-85% drawdown, capitulation |

**Historical drawdowns from peak:**
- 2011: -93%
- 2013-2015: -84%
- 2017-2018: -84%
- 2021-2022: -77%

"Every cycle the drawdown is 'less bad' as more institutional holders are less likely to panic sell."

## Altcoin Season Cycle
**The rotation sequence within a bull market:**
1. **Bitcoin leads** (3-6 months): Institutional quality, clearest narrative
2. **Ethereum follows** (2-4 months): Smart contract platform, DeFi catalyst
3. **Large-cap altcoins** (2-3 months): SOL, AVAX, BNB, etc.
4. **Mid-cap altcoins** (1-2 months): Sector leaders in DeFi, gaming, L2
5. **Small-cap/meme coins** (weeks): Maximum speculation, maximum risk
6. **Total market peaks** — then everything dumps together

**Altcoin Season Index:**
- 75+ out of 100 = Altcoin Season (altcoins outperforming BTC)
- Below 25 = Bitcoin Season
- Rising BTC.D (dominance) = money flowing TO Bitcoin
- Falling BTC.D = money flowing FROM Bitcoin TO altcoins

## Fear & Greed Index Cycle Application
**Crypto Fear & Greed Index (0-100):**
- 0-25 = Extreme Fear
- 26-49 = Fear
- 50 = Neutral
- 51-74 = Greed
- 75-100 = Extreme Greed

**Trading rules:**
- Extreme Fear (< 20) + MVRV < 1 = generational buy opportunity
- Extreme Greed (> 80) sustained = distribution zone
- "Be greedy when others are fearful, fearful when others are greedy" — Buffett applied to crypto

## Bitcoin Dominance Cycle
**BTC.D = Bitcoin market cap / Total crypto market cap**

| BTC.D | Market Phase |
|-------|-------------|
| Rising above 55% | Bitcoin season, altcoins underperforming |
| 45-55% | Transition zone |
| Falling below 45% | Altcoin season beginning |
| Below 40% | Late altcoin season, maximum speculation |

**Historically:** BTC.D peaks in bear market/early bull, bottoms at cycle peaks when altcoins are most speculative.

## Crypto and Traditional Market Correlations

**Short-term (days/weeks):** High correlation to Nasdaq/risk assets
- Risk-off events → crypto sells harder than equities
- Fed rate decisions → immediate crypto reaction

**Medium-term (months):** Correlation to global liquidity
- M2 money supply expansion → crypto outperforms
- Dollar strengthening → crypto underperforms

**Long-term (years):** Crypto uncorrelated to any asset
- Bitcoin has outperformed all assets over 5-year periods despite volatility
- Correlation breaks down during peak bull markets (crypto alone goes parabolic)

## Crypto Credit Cycle (within each 4-year cycle)
Leverage builds and collapses:
1. **Recovery:** Low leverage, organic buying, negative funding
2. **Expansion:** Leverage builds, positive funding, OI rising
3. **Speculation:** High leverage, very positive funding, new products
4. **Euphoria:** Maximum leverage, extreme funding, retail in
5. **Collapse:** Cascade liquidations, funding crashes negative, forced selling
6. **Deleveraging:** OI collapses, realized losses peak → BOTTOM

## Key Cycle Timing Indicators
1. **Days since last halving:** 0-6 months post-halving = patient; 12-24 months = acceleration possible
2. **MVRV ratio:** Position within cycle (see on-chain guide)
3. **Fear & Greed Index:** Sentiment extremes = contrarian signals
4. **Bitcoin dominance trend:** Rising = early cycle; falling = late cycle
5. **Altcoin/BTC ratio:** New all-time highs = late cycle excess
6. **Google Trends for "Bitcoin":** Spike = retail entry = late cycle

## Bear Market Survival Framework
- Peak to trough: Expect 70-85% drawdown
- Duration: 12-24 months typically
- Capitulation: When crypto headlines disappear from mainstream media
- Recovery signal: MVRV below 1.0, exchange reserves declining, LTH accumulating
- Dollar cost averaging into bear market = historically optimal strategy

## The "Crypto Is Dead" Pattern
Every bear market produces "Bitcoin is dead" headlines. Historical death count:
- 2011: 1 major "Bitcoin is dead" article
- 2013-2015: 15 articles
- 2017-2018: 300+ articles
- 2021-2022: Mainstream media extensive coverage

Pattern: More "Bitcoin is dead" coverage = closer to the bottom.
