## Wright's Law Applied to Bitcoin
Wright's Law: For every cumulative doubling of units produced, costs fall by a consistent percentage. Applied to Bitcoin:
- As more people use Bitcoin, transaction costs fall (Lightning Network)
- As hash rate grows, security increases exponentially
- Network effects compound: value grows as square of users (Metcalfe's Law)
- Adoption S-curve: slow start, explosive middle, saturation at global scale
