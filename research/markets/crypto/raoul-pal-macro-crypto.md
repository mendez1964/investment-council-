## The Everything Code
**The single most important framework Raoul uses:**

Global M2 money supply → leads all risk assets by 6-12 months
When central banks print money → liquidity flows into risk assets
When central banks tighten → liquidity drains from risk assets
Crypto is the HIGHEST beta expression of this liquidity cycle

**The sequence:**
1. Global M2 expands (central banks ease)
2. Bond prices rise
3. Equities rise (6-12 months after M2)
4. Crypto rises (6-12 months after equities, most leveraged)
5. Crypto gives maximum returns at peak liquidity expansion
6. Reversal happens in same order

**Monitoring:** Track global M2 on TradingView (M2SL + ECB + BOJ + PBOC)

## The ISM Manufacturing Cycle
ISM Manufacturing PMI (Institute for Supply Management):
- Above 50 = expansion = risk-on
- Below 50 = contraction = risk-off
- Turning points signal risk asset direction changes

**Crypto correlation:**
- ISM turning up from below 50 → next 12-18 months favorable for crypto
- ISM at 55+ and rolling over → be cautious, cycle may be topping
- Most reliable leading indicator for crypto alongside M2

## DXY Dollar Strength Inverse Correlation
Bitcoin and DXY have strong inverse correlation:
- DXY rising = dollar strengthening = global liquidity tightening = BAD for crypto
- DXY falling = dollar weakening = global liquidity expanding = GOOD for crypto
- Watch DXY trend: sustained downtrend = fuel for crypto bull market
- DXY at 10-year highs = be patient, wait for reversal
