# PlanB — Stock-to-Flow Bitcoin Valuation Framework

## Background
Anonymous Dutch institutional investor. Creator of the Stock-to-Flow (S2F) model for Bitcoin — the most influential quantitative Bitcoin price model. Has predicted Bitcoin price ranges with remarkable accuracy across multiple halvings.

## Core Philosophy
Bitcoin's value comes from its scarcity. Stock-to-Flow (S2F) measures scarcity: existing supply divided by new annual production. Bitcoin halvings predictably reduce new supply, creating measurable scarcity increases. Scarcity + demand = price appreciation. On-chain data confirms or denies price models.

## Stock-to-Flow Model Explained

**Formula:** S2F = Stock (total existing supply) / Flow (annual new production)

| Asset | Stock | Flow | S2F Ratio | Price Implication |
|-------|-------|------|-----------|-------------------|
| Silver | ~1.5M tons | ~25K tons/yr | 60 | $25/oz |
| Gold | ~190K tons | ~3,300 tons/yr | 58 | $1,900/oz |
| Bitcoin (pre-2024) | ~19.7M BTC | ~328K BTC/yr | 60 | Same as gold |
| Bitcoin (post-2024 halving) | ~19.7M BTC | ~164K BTC/yr | **120** | **2x gold scarcity** |

**The model's prediction:** As S2F ratio rises, market cap rises proportionally. Bitcoin's S2F doubles every halving.

- Phase 2: valued like collectible

Each phase transition = step change in valuation.

## MVRV Ratio — Cycle Top/Bottom Indicator
**Market Value to Realized Value (MVRV)**
- Market Value = current price × circulating supply
- Realized Value = price each coin last moved × its quantity (cost basis of network)
- MVRV = Market Value / Realized Value

| MVRV | Signal |
|------|--------|
| > 3.5 | **DANGER ZONE** — historically marks cycle tops |
| 2.0-3.5 | Bull market, caution increasing |
| 1.0-2.0 | Fair value range |
| < 1.0 | **OPPORTUNITY ZONE** — historically marks cycle bottoms |
| < 0.8 | Extreme undervaluation — generational buy |

## Realized Price
**The most important support level in Bitcoin:**
- Average cost basis of every Bitcoin on the network
- When price = realized price → most holders at breakeven
- Price below realized price = capitulation territory
- Price returning to realized price = strong support historically
- Currently ~$35,000-45,000 range (updates with each cycle)

## Thermocap Multiple
Measures price vs total mining revenue ever paid:
- High multiple = speculative premium
- Low multiple = fundamental undervaluation
- Historically: buying when thermocap multiple < 10 returns 100x+

## On-Chain Confirmation Metrics PlanB Watches
1. **Days to next halving** — most predictable bullish catalyst
2. **MVRV ratio current position** — over/under valued
3. **Realized price distance** — how far below/above cost basis
4. **Hash rate trend** — miner conviction (miners wouldn't invest if bearish)
5. **Exchange BTC reserves** — declining = accumulation
6. **Long-term holder supply** — percentage of supply unmoved 155+ days

## PlanB Alert Signals
🟢 **Bullish:**
- MVRV ratio < 1.0 (below realized price)
- Price at or below realized price
- Hash rate making new all-time highs
- Long-term holder supply increasing
- <180 days to next halving

🔴 **Bearish/Caution:**
- MVRV ratio > 3.5
- Price >3x above Stock-to-Flow model line
- Hash rate sudden significant decline (>20%)
- Long-term holders beginning to distribute

## Halving Cycle Phases
**Using historical data:**
- **Pre-halving (0-12 months before):** Accumulation phase, price typically rises 50-200% in anticipation
- **Post-halving (0-6 months after):** Consolidation, market digests new supply dynamics
- **Expansion (6-18 months post):** Price acceleration as supply shock hits market
- **Blow-off top (18-24 months post):** Euphoria, MVRV peaks above 3.5
- **Bear market (2-3 years):** Reset, accumulate

## When to Apply This Framework
- Bitcoin valuation questions ("is Bitcoin expensive right now?")
- Halving cycle analysis and timing
- Long-term price target assessment
- On-chain confirmation of price moves
- Cycle top and bottom identification
- Whether current price is above or below fair value model
